How It Works

Inspired by the rubber duck debugging method, CodeQuacker helps you explain your coding problems—either by typing or speaking—and provides AI-driven suggestions to guide you to a solution. Articulating your problem is the first step to fully understanding and solving it!

Who Are We?

We're a group of four passionate college students with diverse interests, united by our love for education, debugging, and coding. Our goal is to make learning through problem-solving fun and accessible to all. Our values are rooted in collaboration, curiosity, and continuous learning. We believe that explaining your thoughts is key to mastering complex concepts.

Key Features

• Google BreadBoard integration for specialized debugging
• MySQL-based login system to save past queries
• Speech-to-text for verbal debugging
• AI-generated practice problems based on debugging history

Rewards System

The more coding problems you solve with CodeQuacker, the more points you earn! Points can be redeemed for rewards like class extra credit, customized ducks, or other fun items. As you complete AI-generated lessons and improve your coding skills, your progress is recognized through rewards that keep you motivated and engaged in learning.
